Aesthetic Nut

Aesthetic Nut is an authentic vegetarian barbecue and North India restaurant and one of the flagship brands of the Sankalp Group. With its humble beginnings in Hosur in 2023, the Group today has more than 5+ successfully operating restaurants across the globe. The group now is a highly diversified entity with interests in Hospitality, Packaged Foods, Manufacturing & Distribution, Exports, Hoteliering and Real Estate.
